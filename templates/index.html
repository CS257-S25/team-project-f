Welcome to StreamSearch

StreamSearch helps you find movies and TV shows based on actor names, categories, and release years.

How to Use StreamSearch:

  • Actor: Enter the name of an actor to find movies or shows they appear in. Example: /actor/Emma Stone
  • Category: Search by genre or category (e.g., Comedy, Action, Drama). Example: /category/Comedy
  • Year: Filter results to show movies or shows released on or after a specified year. Example: /year/2010
